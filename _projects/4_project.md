### What is [Triple II](https://get-triple.com/prototype)?

**Triple II** is an initiative to design health insurance that actually fits the realities of gig work: variable income, fluctuating hours, and changing eligibility. We’re building tools to make coverage simpler to access and better aligned with gig workers. 

### Why Triple II?

The gig economy has grown quickly, but health coverage still assumes a traditional job: stable hours, a single employer, and predictable benefits. For many independent workers, that mismatch can mean gaps in care, financial stress, or losing coverage when work patterns change.

> **The approach:** Triple II focuses on portability, affordability, and tailoring coverage to variable income and work patterns while connecting people to plans and resources that make sense for them.
